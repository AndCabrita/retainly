FAQ

Questions & answers

Do I need to change my existing cancel button code?
▼
No. You paste one JavaScript snippet into your site's <head> or <body>, then tell Retainly the CSS selector of your cancel button. We handle everything from there — intercepting the click, showing the modal, and communicating with Stripe if the customer accepts an offer.
How does the Stripe integration work?
▼
You connect your Stripe account via Stripe Connect (OAuth). Retainly gets read/write access to subscriptions only — to apply discounts, activate pauses, or confirm cancellations. We never touch your payouts or financial data.
What if my customers are on different subscription plans?
▼
Retainly reads the customer's current plan from Stripe and can show different offers per plan. You configure the logic in your dashboard — e.g., only show a 20% discount to customers on the paid plan, show a downgrade to the free plan for trial users.
Is this compliant with GDPR?
▼
Yes. We only store exit survey responses and metadata (plan, timestamp, outcome). No PII unless you pass it to us explicitly. Data is stored in EU-region Supabase infrastructure. We provide a DPA on request.
What's the difference between Retainly and Churnkey?
▼
Churnkey is excellent and very feature-rich — but it starts at $250/month and is targeted at teams doing $50K+ MRR. Retainly is designed for indie founders and small SaaS teams doing $1K–$20K MRR who need the core functionality at a price that makes sense.
